What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that ensures transparency, security, and decentralization in transactions. It records information in blocks, linked together in chronological order, making it nearly impossible to alter or hack the system.

Key Features of Blockchain

  • Decentralization: No single authority controls the network, ensuring transparency and fairness.
  • Immutability: Once data is recorded, it cannot be changed or deleted.
  • Security: Data is encrypted and stored across a distributed network, making it highly secure.
  • Smart Contracts: Self-executing contracts with predefined rules for automating transactions.

Applications of Blockchain

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing industries such as finance, healthcare, supply chain, and more. Here are some key applications:

  1. Cryptocurrencies: The most popular use of blockchain is in cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
  2. Supply Chain Management: Blockchain ensures transparency and traceability in supply chains.
  3. Digital Identity: Securely manage and verify personal identities using blockchain.
  4. Healthcare: Store and share medical records securely and efficiently.
